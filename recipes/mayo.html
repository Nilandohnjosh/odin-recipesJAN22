Mayo Chicken

Description

This is a recipe focusing on the marvelous creation of the delicious gourmet meal, the Nila Supreme Mayo Chicken!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Mayonnaise
  • 2 Cloves garlic, crushed.
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 whole chicke, cut into 6 pieces
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
